## **Objective**
Analyze website performance data related to the Grammys to understand traffic patterns, user engagement, and channel effectiveness, and to identify opportunities for improving marketing impact and website performance.

---

## **Data**
Website analytics data including:
- Traffic volume (sessions, page views)
- User engagement metrics
- Traffic sources
- Page-level performance  
across a defined time period.

---

## **Tools**
- **Excel**
  - Data cleaning and preparation  
  - Pivot tables and calculated metrics  
  - Charts and summary dashboards  

---

## **Analysis Overview**
The analysis focused on how users interact with the website and how different traffic sources contribute to overall performance. Pivot tables and visualizations were used to compare engagement across channels, identify trends over time, and highlight areas of strong and weak performance.

---

## **Key Insights**
- Identified periods of **peak website traffic and engagement**, highlighting when users are most active  
- Determined which **traffic sources drive higher-quality engagement**, not just volume  
- Flagged pages and traffic segments with **lower engagement**, indicating optimization opportunities  

---

## **Business Impact**
Insights from this analysis can support:
- **Improved marketing strategy** by prioritizing high-performing traffic channels  
- **Content optimization** based on user engagement patterns  
- **Website performance improvements** aimed at increasing retention and effectiveness  

---

## **Deliverables**
- Excel workbook containing cleaned data, pivot tables, and analysis  
- Visual summaries and charts highlighting key findings
